ScamShield AI

Protecting Seniors from Online Scams

Our team developed a mobile application prototype (UI) aimed at helping seniors identify and avoid online scams. The solution uses AI-powered URL and message detection to classify content as safe or suspicious, drawing on a continually updated database of known scam patterns. Users can paste links or text messages into the app to receive instant safety feedback, along with highlighted scam indicators for learning and awareness.

Key features include:

  • URL Safety Checker: Detects and flags malicious links before users click them.
  • Message Scam Detector: Analyzes text for scam patterns and keywords using trained ML models.
  • International Number Blocking: Reduces risk from overseas scam calls.
  • Report Function: Allows suspicious numbers to be flagged and shared with the community.

The system was built around web scraping, data curation, feature extraction, and supervised machine learning, paired with a user-friendly interface tailored for seniors. This project directly addressed pain points such as low digital literacy, lack of real-time scam support, and vulnerability to impersonation scams.

LifeSync

Smart, Collaborative, Healthy Eating

Our team conceptualized LifeSync, a mobile application aimed at helping youths and households build healthier eating habits through AI-powered meal analysis, personalized recommendations, and social accountability. This hackathon project was in the ideation stage, and we developed a high-fidelity UI prototype using Adobe XD, along with a proposed technical framework for future implementation.

Key features include:

  • Snap a Meal: Take a photo of your meal and receive instant AI-generated nutritional feedback.
  • AI-Generated Recipes: Tailored to dietary needs such as vegan, halal, and gluten-free.
  • Meal Logging: Track eating habits and monitor progress over time.
  • Smart Grocery Lists: Provide real-time health nudges and suggest healthier alternatives.
  • Gamification: Collaborate and compete with friends or family to stay motivated.
  • AI Chatbot: Offer health tips, recipe ideas, and answer food-related queries.

While not yet a working product, our proposed tech stack included React Native or Flutter for the frontend, Node.js and Express for the backend, TensorFlow/PyTorch for AI-based meal recognition, and Prometheus/Grafana for monitoring. LifeSync stands out for combining AI nutrition analysis, sustainability features, and household collaboration into a single platform.

Internship

RefundFlow+

Refund Management App

A Power Apps solution developed to digitize and streamline Mandai Wildlife Group’s internal refund process, integrated with SharePoint and Power Automate. The app guides requests through three structured stages — Raised Forms, Verification, and Approval — before routing them to the Finance team. Staff can submit, track, and update requests, while role-based permissions ensure that only authorized personnel can verify or approve refunds.

The backend automation, built with Power Automate, streamlines operations by:

  • Sending automated email notifications during submission, verification, and approval stages
  • Routing requests to the correct department or reviewer based on approval logic
  • Notifying the Finance team when a refund is ready for processing

This solution improves turnaround time, enhances transparency, reduces paper usage, and minimizes manual errors in the refund approval workflow.

Find It

Lost & Found Management App

A Power Apps solution designed to help staff efficiently manage lost and found items, fully integrated with SharePoint and Power Automate. The app includes four main modules: Report Lost Item, Report Found Item, Lost Items List, and Found Items List. Staff can file and update reports, search records, schedule collection appointments, and discard unclaimed items after a set period.

The backend automation, built with Power Automate, streamlines operations by:

  • Generating monthly discard reports
  • Notifying security staff of same-day collections
  • Sending automated emails to guests after a lost item report is submitted

This solution improves operational efficiency, enhances guest experience, and ensures accurate tracking of lost and found inventory.

About Me

I’m someone who finds joy in figuring things out — whether it’s fixing a UI flow that doesn’t quite work or finding a smarter way to handle data behind the scenes. What started as a curiosity for how apps are built has grown into a passion for creating digital tools that are both functional and delightful to use.

I enjoy the process of building — from sketching out ideas and prototyping to writing clean code and iterating based on feedback. I thrive in collaborative environments where ideas are shared openly, and I value empathy just as much as technical skill when designing solutions.

While I’m still early in my journey, I’ve learned that the most meaningful projects are the ones that make someone’s day easier, more intuitive, or just a little more enjoyable. That’s the kind of work I hope to keep doing — solving problems with purpose, creativity, and a people-first mindset.
